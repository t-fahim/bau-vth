# Bangladesh Agricultural University Veterinary Teaching Hospital (bau-vth)

## Overview
The **Bangladesh Agricultural University Veterinary Teaching Hospital(bau-vth)** is a **web-based application** developed to modernize and enhance the management of patient records and treatment history at the **Bangladesh Agricultural University Veterinary Teaching Hospital**.  

This system provides a **centralized digital platform** that replaces manual processes, improving **data accuracy, accessibility, and operational efficiency** while supporting the hospital’s academic and clinical mission.

---

## Features
- **Appointment & Visit Management**  
  Schedule, track, and update appointments efficiently.

- **Dashboard & Analytics**  
  View daily activity summaries and key system metrics in a user-friendly dashboard.

- **Reporting**  
  Generate basic reports such as visit summaries, treatment logs, and medicine inventory insights.

- **Search & Retrieval**  
  Quickly search and retrieve patient and owner data using dynamic filters.

- **User Authentication & Roles**  
  Secure access control with roles for administrators, doctors, and receptionists.

| Component | Technology |
